# Learning plan for 2026

Guidelines:
- 3 weekly projects per month, they cannot be continued during the next week (cut-off date). If something should be studied longer, another week can be allocated while planning the next unplanned month.
- We have two levels of planning: Q1, Q2, Q3 and Q4 focus areas
- Monthly planning (3 one-week goals and a cool-down week without topic). We only have monthly plan 1 month ahead.

Topics
- The goal is to upgrade my knowledge in IT engineering with the advancements of AI.
- Focus areas
  - software development methodologies, mainly .NET environment
  - Python and VBA scripting skills
  - Most common, simple website development approaches, when supported by generative AI.
  - Automation tools in a corporate environment: M365 office macros, n8n and similar automations, power automate
  - I prefer code based solutions in contrast to no-code solutions but when a no-code solution is very common, that is interesting as well.
